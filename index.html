WaltSin Music EPK Contact WaltSin Old Spells & New Beginnings Songs that bleed deep & heal dirty Listen Book Now Scroll ✦ WaltSin Walt Sin is an Australian multi-instrumentalist and singer-songwriter — a theatrical mourner, a raging sermoniser, a very drunk nuisance. Raised on hymns and running on bass, he builds songs from broken things, moving through folk, roots, blues, funk and hillbilly rock. "As the lingering corpse flower stinks sweetest after years spent rotting, so does the nectar darken with time. So can a song brew in the shadows."
